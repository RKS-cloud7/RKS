A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2015 by Master Chef Rajesh Kumar, Royal Thali emerged from a vision to redefine Indian fine dining. With over 25 years of culinary expertise spanning the finest kitchens of India and abroad, Chef Rajesh brought together traditional wisdom and modern techniques to create something truly extraordinary.

What started as an intimate dining space for 20 guests has evolved into a culinary destination that welcomes over 500 discerning diners daily. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as one of the city's premier restaurants, yet we remain dedicated to the personal touch that made us special from day one.

Our Journey of Excellence

2015

The Royal Beginning

Royal Thali opened its doors in the heart of the city, introducing an innovative thali concept that combined traditional Indian flavors with contemporary presentation.

2025

Digital Excellence

Launched our state-of-the-art online ordering platform and virtual cooking classes, bringing Royal Thali's culinary excellence to homes worldwide.

Our Culinary Philosophy

At Royal Thali, we believe that great cuisine is an art form that brings people together. Our philosophy centers on three core principles: authenticity, innovation, and sustainability. We source the finest ingredients from trusted local farmers and artisans, ensuring every dish tells a story of India's rich culinary heritage.

Our chefs blend time-honored techniques with contemporary presentation, creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate both tradition and modernity. Every thali we serve is a masterpiece of balance, nutrition, and flavor harmony.
